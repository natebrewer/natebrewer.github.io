About Me

Hello! My name is Nathan Brewer, and I'm a sophomore at Swarthmore College studying Engineering. I was born and raised in Staunton, Virginia in the Blue Ridge Mountains, and I went to high school at The Cambridge School of Weston just outside Boston, Massachusetts.

I have worked since October of 2024 in Dr. Carr Everbach's biomimetic robotics lab, replicating zooplankton locomotion methods for planetary exploration robotics. I am also the co-founder and team lead of Swarthmore Combat Robotics (check out our Antweight bot on the main page), as well as a team lead for Swarthmore Rocketry. Outside of my STEM interests, I volunteer for the Swarthmore chapter of Youth for Innocence and draw portraits/caricatures.
This site will likely be the most up-to-date representation of my work, though I can realistically update it a few times per month. If you want to get in contact with me for any reason, feel free to reach out to nbrewer1@swarthmore.edu! I should respond within a day.
